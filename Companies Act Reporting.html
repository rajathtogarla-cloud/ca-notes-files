Inquiry on Specific Matters

Section 143(1)

The auditor shall inquire into the following matters. Adverse comments must be reported with reasons.

Loans & Advances

  • Are they properly secured?
  • Are terms prejudicial to the company or its members?

Book Entries

Whether transactions represented by book entries are prejudicial to the company’s interests.

Sale of Securities

Whether shares/securities are sold at a price less than the purchase price.
(Excludes Banking/Investment Companies)

Loans as Deposits

Whether loans/advances are incorrectly shown as deposits.

Personal Expenses

Whether personal expenses are charged to the revenue account.

Shares Allotted for Cash

  • Has cash actually been received?
  • Is the position in books/balance sheet correct and not misleading?

Reporting on Frauds

Section 143(12)
Fraud < 1 Crore
  • Report to: Audit Committee / Board
  • Details: Nature, Amount, Parties involved
  • Disclosure: Board's Report must include details + remedial actions
Fraud ≥ 1 Crore
1

Within 2 Days

Report to Board/Audit Committee seeking reply.

2

Wait 45 Days

For reply from Board/Committee.

3

Within 15 Days

Forward report + reply (ADT-4) to Secretary, MCA.

Penalties for Non-Compliance
Listed Companies ₹ 5 Lakh
Other Companies ₹ 1 Lakh
